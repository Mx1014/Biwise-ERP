Two-Tier marketing in Odoo Extension for Odoo Affiliate Management

You can easily manage your Two-Tier marketing in Odoo Affiliate Commissions

Two-Tier marketing Extension in ODOO Affiliate Management for E-Commerce store helps admin to set commission for Two-tier referral affiliates. Now your affiliate can earn commission not only by the sales generated by his marketing efforts but also from the sales generated by their referrals. Affiliate Two-tier commission is one of the most effective methods for significantly increasing the size of an online business and has proved to be a cost-efficient, measurable method of delivering long-term results.

This Extension Allows

  • Real-time reporting.
  • Two level Referral Tracking with graphical tree view.
  • Unlimited Affiliates.
  • Automatic invoicing for Two-Level Referral.
  • Set the different commission programs for Two-tier Referral.
  • Easy to handle admin options.
  • Quick and Easy Installation.
